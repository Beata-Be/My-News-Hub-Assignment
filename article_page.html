Our Secret Pumpkin Spice Recipe

It costs only $5.50 and contains real pumpkin!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • 2 tablespoons pumpkin puree
  • 1-2 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice
  • 1-2 teaspoons sugar or sweetener (optional)
  • 1 shot espresso or 1/2 cup strong brewed coffee

Instructions

  1. In a small saucepan, heat the milk over medium heat until hot but not boiling.
  2. Stir in the pumpkin puree, pumpkin pie spice, and sugar until well combined and smooth.
  3. Use a frother, whisk, or blender to create foam in the mixture.
  4. Pour the espresso or coffee into a mug.
  5. Top with the spiced pumpkin milk and serve immediately.
